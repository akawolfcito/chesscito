## Problem *
**Preview:**
e.g. Climate change is a serious problem that is affecting our planet and we are building a platform that connects people with similar interests.

Learning chess fundamentals is often too slow, abstract, and intimidating for mobile-first users. Most tools are either long-form courses or full chess games that create high friction for beginners. There is also little verifiable signal of progress across lightweight learning experiences.

## Solution *
**Preview:**
We are solving this problem by building a platform that connects people with similar interests.

Chesscito solves this by delivering fast, focused pre-chess missions designed for short mobile sessions. Players get immediate feedback through interactive board mechanics, clear objective loops, and progression markers. On Celo, key progress actions can be recorded as low-friction onchain events (scores, badges), enabling portable and verifiable achievement history.
